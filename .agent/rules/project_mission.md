# Project Mission & Vision: bud-zdorov

This document defines the purpose and core values of the project. Every piece of content, every UI element, and every new feature should align with these principles.

## 1. Our Mission
To help people find clear, actionable, and scientifically sound information about their health. The goal is to guide users toward harmony through nutrition, mindfulness, and movement.

## 2. Professionalism & Credibility
- **Expertise First**: Users must feel they are interacting with a highly experienced professional. 
- **Evidence-Based**: We prioritize facts and professional experience over health trends.
- **Trust**: Every design choice should reinforce trust (clean layouts, professional imagery, clear credentials).

## 3. Communication & Feedback
- **Always Responsive**: The project emphasizes that the professional is always available for feedback.
- **Empathy**: Our tone is supportive and empathetic, never judgmental or overly clinical.
- **Support**: Users should feel they are not alone in their journey.

## 4. Tone of Voice (ToV)
- **Primary**: Professional, Reliable, and Wise.
- **Secondary**: Nuturing, Encouraging, and Accessible.
- **Anti-Patterns**: Avoid using slang, hype-y marketing language, or making unverified medical claims.
